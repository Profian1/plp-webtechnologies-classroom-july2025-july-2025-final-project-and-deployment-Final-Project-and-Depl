About Us

Our Story

At Designer Carpets, our journey began in 2005 with a simple belief: that every home deserves a touch of craftsmanship and personalization. What started as a small workshop has grown into a dedicated team of artisans passionate about creating custom-designed rugs and carpets that reflect the unique style and personality of each client.

Over the years, we've refined our craft, blending traditional techniques with modern innovation to deliver pieces that are not just beautiful, but timeless. Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction drives everything we do.
